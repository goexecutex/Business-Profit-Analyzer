# GoExecuteX Insights 📊
### Sales & Profit Analysis Dashboard for Small Businesses

---

## The Problem

Most small businesses know what sells. But not every business knows what actually makes profit.

A product can sell 100 units a day and still drain your margins. GoExecuteX Insights helps business owners see exactly which products are driving profit, which are underperforming, and where their money is quietly leaking.

---

## What It Does

Upload your sales data (CSV or Excel) and instantly get:

- **KPI Overview** — Total revenue, total profit, average profit margin, transaction count
- **Top Profitable Items** — Which products make you the most money
- **Best Sellers** — Which products sell the most by volume
- **Lowest Margin Items** — Products that sell well but barely break even
- **Hidden Profit Leaks** — High-volume items with dangerously thin margins
- **Daily / Weekly Sales Trends** — How your revenue moves over time
- **Smart Recommendations** — Which products to promote, reprice, review, or remove
